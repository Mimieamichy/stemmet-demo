Our programs

Four subjects. One acronym. Real projects.

Every program maps to a letter in STEM METS, same order as the badge on our door, because that's genuinely how we built the curriculum.

S — SCIENCE

Creative Science + Engineering

Screen-free coding and robotics fundamentals. Kids build simple machines and learn cause-and-effect before code ever enters the picture.

Ages 3–9

T — TECHNOLOGY

Creative Tech with Ozaria

Real coding, digital literacy and online safety through story-driven quests, for kids who've outgrown drag-and-drop blocks.

Ages 8–13

E — ENGINEERING

Smart Systems Camp

Kids build their own bots, wire up sensors, and test them for real, figuring out how a machine knows when to stop or turn.

Ages 7–13

M — MATH

Creative Design & Logic

2D/3D design and animation in Blender, spatial reasoning and geometry disguised as building worlds and characters.

Ages 9–13

The learning path

A track for every stage, ages 3 to 13.

Programs are grouped by developmental stage, not just age, each one builds directly on the skills from the last.

01

Stage one

Explorers

Ages 3–6

Sensory play with real materials, circuits, blocks and shapes that teach cause and effect long before a screen is involved.

02

Stage two

Builders

Ages 7–9

First robots, first code blocks, first "it actually works" moment, group projects that reward trying again.

03

Stage three

Innovators

Ages 10–13

Independent builds, real coding languages, and 3D design work that kids take home and keep improving.
